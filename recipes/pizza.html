Pizza Recipe

Why Pizza?

What to do when your 8-year old nephew comes to visit? Make pizza, of course!

Ingredients

  • tomato sauce
  • Fresh soft mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Make and knead the pizza dough
  2. Preheat the pizza stone
  3. Flatten the dough ball, and stretch out into a round.
  4. Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings
  5. Slide pizza into the oven for baking
